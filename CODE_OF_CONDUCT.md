# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being kind and welcoming to newcomers and first-time contributors
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Trolling, or sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainers on [Discord](https://discord.gg/gqhDWfZbgU) or at
`conduct@zetic.ai`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly
and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of
the reporter of any incident.
